Save and enhance the lives of others

We help individuals accomplish something truly remarkable: giving the gift of life through blood, organ, tissue, or stem cell donation to help those in need.

Dedicated to preserving life

Our ongoing commitment is to enhance service efficiency, accessibility for patients and donors, and collaborate with the NHS to reduce hospital costs.

Over 300,000 donors a year

We offer a secure, accessible and cost-effective blood service through our optimised supply chain management
